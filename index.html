Open-Source PyMOL


PyMOL(TM) is a molecular graphics system with an embedded Python interpreter designed for real-time visualization and rapid generation of high-quality molecular graphics images and animations. It can also perform many other valuable tasks (such as editing PDB files) to assist you in your research. PyMOL is a USER-SPONSORED molecular visualization system on an OPEN-SOURCE foundation.


Available for Windows, macOS and Linux.

What is Open-Source PyMOL?
